About GenAI

Generative AI refers to AI (artificial intelligence) that is capable of automatically generating content with a high degree of similarity to human-created content, making it difficult to distinguish between the two. It is a type of machine-learning algorithm to generate models to understand those patterns and features in large datasets which it then uses to generate new content. Humans commonly use generative AI for various types of content creation, such as images, video, music, text, etc. Since there is currently no clear legal regulation of this generative content, while people are enjoying the generated content by generative AI, copyright-related issues may arise.
