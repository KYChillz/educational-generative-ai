Concerns of Generative AI

As Generative AI continuous to advance, it brings with the concerns that ripple through society, technology, or even ethics.

Generative AI can create indistinguishable from the works of human which is awe-inspiring and alarming.

Generative AI has greatly contributed to society, but at the same time, it is equally crucial not to overlook the negative implications and concerns it has. (Golda et al., 2024)

So...

Since Generative AI allows content creation and needs a large data set to operate, the copyright and privacy of internet users' data may cause potential ramifications. The copyright issues between the user, Generative AI company (OpenAI), data itself is complex. There are debates where Generative AI uses unauthorised data to generate content, making the company liable for using copyrighted data. Some argue that AI acts as a tool, making it not liable.

There are various ongoing efforts to detect AI-generated media. In early 2023, OpenAI released a work-in-progress classifier to distinguish between machine-generated and human-written text to help mitigate concerns about running automated misinformation campaigns, among other problems. ( Dhamani, 2024, chapter 4)

Whatever the case maybe, if considering using Generative AI for any personal or commercial use, users should be mindful while using this technology.
